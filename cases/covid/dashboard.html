Is the conditional probability p(HSM cluster | Lab Leak, Wuhan) closer to 1% (making HSM evidence negligible) or closer to 0.01% (making HSM evidence decisive)?

What is at stake

If resolved one way vs the other:

If p(HSM|LL,W) ≥ 1-5%, the claimed 10,000x Bayes factor collapses to <5x, eliminating HSM as decisive evidence and potentially flipping the overall posterior. If p(HSM|LL,W) << 0.1%, the HSM cluster remains the dominant evidence supporting zoonosis with a multi-thousand-fold Bayes factor.

pre_market_circulation

Crux — a disagreement that, if resolved, would shift the debate. The precise question is below (not the short id above).

Did COVID-19 circulate in Wuhan before the HSM cluster emerged in early December 2019, or does exponential growth modeling plus absence of confirmed earlier cases establish that HSM was truly the origin point?

What is at stake

If resolved one way vs the other:

If pre-market circulation occurred, it breaks the tight temporal association between HSM and pandemic origin, opening space for lab leak scenarios with cryptic spread. If circulation before early December is definitively ruled out, it strongly constrains lab leak scenarios and supports HSM as the actual spillover location.

How to settle it (resolution protocol)

Requires: (1) comprehensive seropositivity surveys in Wuhan population outside HSM connections; (2) validation that laboratory testing methods applied to the 92 WHO-investigated cases had sufficient sensitivity; (3) examination of whether early transmission could have followed stochastic/subcritical dynamics rather than consistent exponential growth; (4) analysis of whether abortive transmission chains are epidemiologically plausible.
